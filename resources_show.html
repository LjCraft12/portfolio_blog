General Assembly

Once I knew that I really wanted to look into web development as my career I looked into fast tracking my education in the field and started landing on these places called "bootcamps". They are 12 - 16 week courses usually and are direct job replacements. I was immersed in the world of web coding for 12 weeks from 9am to 6pm or later Monday thru Friday and weekends as well if it was project week. I don't recommend them to everyone because the work load is heavy and the pace of class is SUPER fast paced. But if you can get through it and you can really dedicate your time into practicing, practicing, and more practicing then it might be the place for you.(Can I get a +1 for the oxford comma?)
